Courses

Klym offers a diverse array of classes to take you from waiver to complete comfort on the wall in no time flat.

On The Wall: Basics Climbing

This introductory course will take a new participant and guide them with expert analysis through the basics of climbing. This class is geared towards beginners to V2 climbers.

Intermediate Climbing

This course is all about the technique. Learn hand and foot drills to make working through a problem effortless as your instructors help you better read your future objectives.

Advanced Climbing

This is where things get spicy as they would say. This course seeks to take the climber through the requirements to send V6+ consistently.
