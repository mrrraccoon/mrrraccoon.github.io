Long Fiction

The Mobian Saga

One woman's journey to the brink of her humanity as universal machinations work against her every step as the Solar System falls to the brinks of war.

Will Cemal Tanzari fall to the devices of a corrupt brotherhood bent on planetary starvation and genocide? Or will she muster the strength to fight on and unravel The Brotherhood of Fire's devious plans for solar civil war.

The Bone Creek Collection

Set in the 1860-70s, The Bone Creek Collection follows Donahue, an aging cowboy in the rough and tumble town of Arizona City (Yuma, AZ).

Donahue must face robbers, crooked politicians, rabid animals and the forward unflinching march of progress.

Can he maintain a life of peace or will the harbinger of bad fates call into the darkness for Donahue to go the way of the buffalo?
