About

When not helping guide the climbing community through instruction and training, I like to adventure outdoors and write fiction and meditate.

I'm an avid reader who enjoys history, philosophy, sci-fi, and many others. I collect copies of Dante's works and maintain a growing book collection.

I like plants, cats, and follow the Ch'an Buddhist lineage. I blog in my spare time.

Fiction

Thousands of trees have lost their lives needlessly for me.

Or at least you'd believe it had you known me at the time I first started writing. So, so many years ago all thanks to MTv and Billy Corgan. More specifically the Mellon Collie and the Infinite Sadness album.

There was just something about hearing it on MTv late in 1995 that opened the magical box. I clearly remember sitting in Social Studies and ripping off Bullet With Butterfly Wings. Oh what trash ensued.

Hopefully the things you read here will be marginally better than that.
