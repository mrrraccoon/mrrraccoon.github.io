About

When not helping guide the climbing community through instruction and training, I like to adventure outdoors and write fiction and meditate.

I'm an avid reader who enjoys history, philosophy, sci-fi, and many others. I collect copies of Dante's works and maintain a growing book collection.

I like plants, cats, and follow the Ch'an Buddhist lineage.

Web Development

WebDev is a little weird for me.

I grew up a little before it all took off and had a class in the early days of the Internet. Think Dogpile and Lycos search engines.

Post 9/11 I dabbled in graphic design for web but that fell off too. Covid gave me time to sit down and really focus on what has changed and improved and boy is it a lot.

I'm currently in the process of working through an 54hr course load for full-stack development. This site will improve and streamline over time. Enjoy the hideous design choices to come.

This is a digital scan of the original hand printed negative.
